Commercial Art

Creating visual stories that inspire and engage

Course Description

Commercial Art at BCTHS prepares students for exciting careers in graphic design, advertising, digital media, and visual communication. Students develop creative problem-solving skills while bringing artistic visions to life through industry-standard software and traditional art techniques.

Throughout this major, you'll master the fundamental principles of design while specializing in commercial applications that communicate powerful messages and create compelling visual experiences for diverse audiences.
