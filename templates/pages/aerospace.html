Aerospace Engineering

Designing the future of flight and space exploration

Course Description

Aerospace Engineering at BCTHS prepares students for exciting careers in aircraft and spacecraft design, development, and testing. Students develop critical problem-solving skills while bringing creative solutions to life through hands-on projects and industry-standard software applications.

Throughout this major, you'll master the fundamental concepts of engineering while specializing in aerospace applications that push the boundaries of what's possible in flight and space exploration.
