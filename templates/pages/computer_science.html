Computer Science

Programming the future through code and innovation

Course Description

Computer Science at BCTHS prepares students for exciting careers in software development, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and technology innovation. Students develop logical problem-solving skills while bringing digital solutions to life through modern programming languages and cutting-edge technologies.

Throughout this major, you'll master the fundamental concepts of programming and computer systems while specializing in applications that power our digital world and shape the future of technology.
