Automotive Engineering

Building the future of transportation and mobility

Course Description

Automotive Engineering at BCTHS prepares students for exciting careers in vehicle design, manufacturing, and automotive technology. Students develop critical problem-solving skills while bringing innovative solutions to life through hands-on projects and industry-standard diagnostic equipment.

Throughout this major, you'll master the fundamental concepts of automotive systems while specializing in modern vehicle technologies that drive the future of transportation and sustainable mobility.
